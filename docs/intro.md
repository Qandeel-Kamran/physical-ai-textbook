# Introduction to Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics

Welcome to the comprehensive textbook on Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics. This textbook is organized into four modules covering:

- **Module 1**: ROS 2 Fundamentals & Humanoid Control
- **Module 2**: Simulation & High-Fidelity Digital Twins
- **Module 3**: Learning & Intelligence for Humanoids
- **Module 4**: Capstone & Hardware Implementation

Each module contains multiple chapters that progressively build your understanding of humanoid robotics, from basic ROS 2 concepts to advanced AI implementations.

## About This Textbook

This AI-native textbook provides:

- Interactive elements including quizzes, acknowledgments, and videos
- Real-world examples with ROS 2, Gazebo, Unity, and Isaac Sim
- Hands-on projects and capstone experiences
- Personalization options for different skill levels
- Urdu translation capabilities for broader accessibility
